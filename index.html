Intructions

Below are four crayons. Click on a crayon to add a specific amount of points to your total score. The game will hide this amount until you have gathered the exact amount of crayons the computer has. You must decided which crayons to collect to match the goal number. Once you match, you win and the game will restart. If you go over, the game will shutdown and restart.
